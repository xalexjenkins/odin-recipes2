chili

A beautiful pot of chili with onions, beans pepper and ground beef surely to make your stomachs full

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium yellow onion (diced)
  • 1 pound 90% lean ground beef
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 2 tablespoons ground cumin
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 1 1/2 cups beef broth
  • 1 (15oz) can petite diced tomatoes
  • 1 (16oz) can red kidney beans, drained and rinced
  • 1 (80oz) can tomato sauce

STEPS

  1. Add olive oil to a large soup pot and place it over medium-high heat for two minutes. Add the onion. Cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally
  2. Add the ground beef to the pot. Break it apart with a wooden spoon. Cook for 6-7 minutes. Add the onion. Cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally
  3. Add the chili powder, cumin, sugar, tomato paste, garlic powderm salt, pepper and optional cayenne. Stir until well combined
  4. Add the broth, diced tomatoes (with their juce), drained beans and tomato sauce. Stirll well.
  5. Bring the liquid to a low boil. Then, reduce the heat (low to medium-low) to gently simmer the chili, uncovered, for 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Remove the pot from the heat. Let the chili rest for 5-10 minutes before serving.

NOTES

Reccomend to skip the cayenne pepper if you have young children. Instead opt to just servce bottled hot sauce for people enjoy the spice
